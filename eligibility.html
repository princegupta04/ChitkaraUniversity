Undergraduate Programs

Chitkara University offers a range of undergraduate programs. To be eligible for admission to an undergraduate program, you must have:

  • Completed your 12th grade or equivalent with the required minimum percentage (varies by program)
  • Passed the university's admission test (if applicable)
  • Met any additional program-specific requirements

Postgraduate Programs

For postgraduate programs, eligibility typically includes:

  • A relevant undergraduate degree with a required minimum CGPA or percentage
  • Good scores in standardized tests (if required for the program)
  • Any additional program-specific prerequisites or qualifications

Ph.D. Programs

Admission to Ph.D. programs at Chitkara University generally requires:

  • A master's degree in the relevant field with a minimum CGPA or percentage
  • A research proposal or statement of purpose
  • Performance in the university's admission test or interview (if applicable)

Application Process

For detailed information on the admission process, application deadlines, and required documents, please visit our Admissions page. You can apply online through our application portal.
