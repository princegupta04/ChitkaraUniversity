Welcome to Chitkara University

Chitkara University is a premier institution that welcomes international students from all over the world. Our international programs are designed to provide you with a world-class education and a rich cultural experience.

Why Choose Chitkara University?

Studying abroad at Chitkara University offers numerous advantages:

  • Highly qualified and experienced faculty
  • State-of-the-art facilities and infrastructure
  • Rich cultural diversity and international student community
  • Excellent career opportunities and industry connections
  • Extensive support services for international students

Academic Programs

We offer a wide range of academic programs for international students, including undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral degrees. You can explore our programs in engineering, business, healthcare, sciences, and more.

Admissions

Learn more about our admission requirements and application process. We provide dedicated assistance for international students to make the application process as smooth as possible.

Student Life

Discover the vibrant campus life at Chitkara University. From student clubs and cultural events to sports and recreational activities, there's always something exciting happening on our campus.
