Dr. Ashok K. Chitkara

Dr. Ashok K. Chitkara, one of the visionary founders of Chitkara University, has been a driving force behind our institution's growth. With a Ph.D. in education and a wealth of experience, he has devoted his life to nurturing young minds and shaping the future of education.

Dr. Chitkara's vision and dedication have led to the establishment of a world-class educational institution known for innovation, research, and excellence. He continues to inspire and lead our university towards new horizons.

Dr. Madhu Chitkara

Dr. Madhu Chitkara, co-founder of Chitkara University, is an accomplished educator and a leading light in the field of education. Her extensive experience and commitment to quality education have played a pivotal role in shaping the educational landscape.

Dr. Chitkara's passion for teaching and innovation has been instrumental in the university's development. Her vision and leadership have fostered a dynamic learning environment that prepares students for a changing world.
