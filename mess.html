Welcome to Our Mess Facilities

Chitkara University provides well-organized and hygienic mess facilities to cater to the diverse dietary needs of our students, faculty, and staff. Our mess services are designed to ensure that everyone receives nutritious and delicious meals.

What We Offer

Our mess facilities offer the following:

  • Varied and balanced meal options
  • Fresh and locally sourced ingredients
  • Hygienic food preparation and service
  • Vegetarian and non-vegetarian meal options
  • Special dietary accommodations

Meal Plans

We offer flexible meal plans, including monthly, semester, and yearly options. You can choose a plan that suits your needs and preferences. Our aim is to provide a convenient and cost-effective dining experience for our university community.

Operating Hours

Our mess facilities are open during the following hours:

Breakfast: 7:00 AM - 10:00 AM

Lunch: 12:00 PM - 3:00 PM

Dinner: 6:00 PM - 9:00 PM
