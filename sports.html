Sports and Athletics

Chitkara University offers a wide range of sports and athletic activities to help students stay active and healthy. Our facilities and sports programs include:

  • Indoor and outdoor sports facilities
  • Cricket, football, basketball, tennis, and more
  • Professional coaching and training
  • Inter-university sports competitions

Student Clubs and Organizations

We encourage students to participate in various clubs and organizations to develop skills, socialize, and pursue their interests. Some of our student clubs include:

  • Debating Society
  • Cultural Club
  • Environmental Club
  • Robotics Club
  • Entrepreneurship Club

Cultural and Extracurricular Activities

Chitkara University hosts a wide range of cultural and extracurricular events throughout the year, including:

  • Annual cultural festivals
  • Musical performances and concerts
  • Drama and theater productions
  • Art and photography exhibitions

Getting Involved

To get involved in sports, clubs, or cultural activities, visit our Campus Life and Student Organizations sections. You can also contact our Campus Life Office for more information on how to join or start a club.
