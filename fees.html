Cost of Education

Chitkara University is committed to providing quality education at an affordable cost. Below is an overview of our tuition and fees structure for the current academic year. Please note that these fees are subject to change.

Tuition Fees

Our tuition fees vary by program. The detailed fee structure can be found in our program brochures or on the respective program pages on our website. Tuition fees cover the cost of instruction and academic services.

Other Fees

In addition to tuition, students may be required to pay other fees, including but not limited to:

  • Registration fees
  • Library fees
  • Examination fees
  • Hostel and accommodation fees
  • Transportation fees

Financial Aid and Scholarships

Chitkara University offers various scholarships and financial aid options to eligible students. For detailed information on scholarships and how to apply, please visit our Scholarships page.

Payment Options

We offer multiple payment options, including online payment, bank transfers, and payment plans. Visit our Payment Options page for more information on how to pay your fees.
