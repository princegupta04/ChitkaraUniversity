About Our Hostel

Welcome to the Chitkara University Hostel! Our hostel provides a comfortable and safe living environment for students. We understand the importance of a conducive living space, and our hostel is designed to meet your needs.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a home away from home for our students. We strive to offer a nurturing and inclusive environment where you can focus on your studies, make friends, and create lasting memories.

Facilities

Our hostel offers a wide range of facilities to enhance your stay:

  • Spacious and well-furnished rooms
  • 24/7 security and surveillance for your safety
  • Common study areas to help you concentrate on your studies
  • Dining hall serving nutritious and delicious meals
  • Recreation room with games and entertainment for relaxation
